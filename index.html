About Me

I am a PhD candidate at TU Delft. My research focus is developing Energy-neutral Internet of Things. The research is conducted under the supervision of Prof. Koen Langendoen and Dr. Przemyslaw Pawelczak.

I hold a MSc in Computer science and a BSc in Telecommunication/Electrical engineering from the University of Technology in Baghdad.
